Problem Description

The facility layout in Singapore was designed in 2011 & was duplicated from the US Model. This led to:

1. Less optimised material flow (i.e., Backtracking)

2. Higher material handling costs (i.e., Transportation Costs)

3. Inefficient use of resources

Project Objectives

1. Assess the performance of the existing manufacturing floor

2. Perform optimization methodologies to recommend layout alternernatives

3. Ascertain financial feasibility of recommendations

Computerized Relative Allocation of Facilities Technique (CRAFT)

The heuristic considers pairwise facility exchanges amongst work centers

The layout that minimizes the objective function is selected and the process iterates greedily until no further improvement can be attained
